The Email

What to send, What to expect

Included in the /home page is a link to /signup. After clicking this button a modal will appear with a mailto address. You can use this, or email us directly at lancedhosting@gmail.com!

What to send

Clicking on the email link will automatically fill out your mail programs feilds with the following:
(1) I found you through...
(2) I want my username to be...
(3) I want my email to be...
(4) I want to join because...
(5) SSH Public Key here:

(1) Where did you find us? Google? Twitter? Are you friends with Arya?
(2) Enter a username, perferably all lowercase starting with a character or number
(3) What email do you want associated with this account? This is how you'll communicate with others and receive notifications!
(4) Why do you want to join? Looking for free hosting? Or just for funzies?
(5) How to Guide

You must include all this information to join!

What to expect

You'll get an email back whithin 24 hours concerning your github repository and hosting options!
